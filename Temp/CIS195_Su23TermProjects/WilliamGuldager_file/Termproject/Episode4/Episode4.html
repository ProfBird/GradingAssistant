Star Wars Movie Recap Episodes I Through VI


Episode IV

Rebel carry a secret plan that can defeat the empires weapon. Their ship is invaded by darth vader and we meet Leia, C3P0 and R2D2. Leia is a princess of Alderann and records a message asking Obi-wan for help destroying the empires' weapon. She sends C3P0 and R2D2 in escape pods to find him on Tatooine. The robots get captured and sold to Luke Skywalker a farmer. Luke lives with his aunt and uncle. Luke finds the message on the robots and finds Old Man Ben who turns out to be Obi-wan Kenobi. He gives Luke his fathers lightsaber and they go to Mos Eisley to fimd a pilot to take them to Leia. They find a pilot named Han Solo and he and Chewbakka own the Millennium Falcon. They fly over to where they think Leia is and get trapped in a giant space station they though was a moon. The Empire has the Death Star a massive weapon capable of destroying planets. They rescue Leia, And Obi-wan sacrifices himself to Darth Vader so everyone else can escape. They get the Rebels and luke blows up the death star thanks to the secret plans and saves the day with the power of the force.
