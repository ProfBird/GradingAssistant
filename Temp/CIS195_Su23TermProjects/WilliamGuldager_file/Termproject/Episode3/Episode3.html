Star Wars Movie Recap Episodes I Through VI


Episode III

There is a giant space fight between the seperatists and the republic. Anakin and obiwan board a seperatist ship to save Chancelor Palpatine from Count Dooku. Anakin kills him upon Palpatines urging. They crash land the ship on courasant where anakin meets with padme and finds out she is pregnant. Anakin starts seeing visions of padme dying in childbirth. Palpatine rewards Anakin by putting him on the council of the Jedi Order. But the jedi don't grant him the rank of master and this upsets anakin. He is ordered to spy on palpatine who is his friend. Anakin is losing faith in the jedi. Obi wan hunts and kills General Grievous. Palpatine trues to lure anakin woth storues of sith who could save people from dying. Later Palpatine reveals he is a sith lord known as Darth Sideous. The jedi council try and stop palpatine but anakin steps in and saves him because of his worry for padme. He accepts the dark side of the force storms the jedi temple. And goes to Mustafar to find padme. He has a giant duel with obiwan who snuck on padmes ship. Objwan thought thr jedi were good and anakin had a different point of view. They end the duel with anakin having his limbs chopped off and he burns in lava. Padme gives birth to 2 babies before dying of sadness. The babies are named Luke and Leia. Anakin is saved at the last minute by Palpatine and is forced to wear a robotic suit to stay alive. He is now Darth vader and will help Palpatines new empire rule. Obi-wan gives Leia to the Organa family of Alderann. He gives Luke to live with his aunt and uncle on Tatooine.
