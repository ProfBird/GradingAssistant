Star Wars Movie Recap Episodes I Through VI


Episode II

We start the movie out 10 years after episode 1. Obi wan is a jedi knight and anakin is his padawan. The queen from Naboo is the target of multiple assassination attempts. The jedi counsul assign anakin and obiwan to protect her while she pushes for legislation that would start producing an army to fight the trade federation and the seperatists. Obi wan gets sidetracked tracking one of the assasins. While tracking the assassin obiwan learns about an army that was created for the republic without anyones knowlage. This army was an army of clones. The person cloned was the assassin he was looking for. He fights the assassin and chases him in space for a while. Meanwhile anakin and padme go to naboo where they start to fall in love which is prohibited by the jedi. Anakin gets some scary visions of his mother dying so he murders some sand people and buries his mother. Obi-wan traces the assassin to a planet called geonosis. Senator Palpatine is granted emergency power to become councellor palpatine because of the war. Anakin and padme go to Geonosis to help obi wan and they all get captured. They have a giant fight and the clones show up and save the day. Anakin and padme secretly get married on naboo and the movie ends.
