Dice Roller

This simple dice roller can help you run your game without physical dice. Just choose the type of die to roll, and the quantity, then hit the "Click to Roll" button. Your dice will appear, plus the total of all rolled dice. Note: the color scheme of the die may look a little funny in this design. I chose to model the colors of the dice after those included in the 1977 Basic Dungeons & Dragons set. See image here: Dungeons & Dragons Basic Set.
