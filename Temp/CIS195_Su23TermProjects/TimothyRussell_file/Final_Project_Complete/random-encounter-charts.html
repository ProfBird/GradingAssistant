Random Encounter Charts

This is a collection of random encounter charts I have used in my campaigns. Depending on the circumstances, the probability of an encounter changes, but for ease of use all of these charts have a 50% chance of an encounter. To determine an encounter, choose a environment that the characters are in (i.e. Forest, City), roll a d100, and consult the "Roll" column on the chart.

Forest Random Encounters Table
RollTitleDescription
1-50Nothing HappensThe party travels without interruption.
51-55Spider NestThe party stumbles into a ravine filled with webs and swarming spiders. Navigating through requires Dexterity checks to avoid getting entangled.
56-60Strange GoblinsThe party stumbles upon a group of goblins wearing strange clothes and speaking in an unfamiliar accent. They seem unaggressive. They are led by a hobgoblin named Hargaroth.
61-65Hungry GoblinsA group of 1d4 goblins is walking through the woods, complaining about how hungry they are. They look like they haven’t eaten in days. They won’t hesitate to attack the players for food.
66-70Orphan BearYou see a small brown bear lumber around a tree. It is eating some berries. If the players get too close, it will charge them and attack. It’s an orphan.
71-75Beset CampersA group of people are camping in the woods. They are being attacked by 2d6 giant rats. If players help them defeat the rats, the group of people will reward them with a potion or 2d20 silver pieces.
76-80Fox Hunt1d4+1 gnolls are chasing a fox when the PCs run across them. The fox is actually a novice druid using wildshape for the first time.
81-85Poison PatchPatches of poisonous plants grow in a field of plants that look very similar. The plants can be used as an ingredient to make potions of poison, but if touched they poison a character for 1d4 hours if they fail a DC 13 Constitution saving throw.
86-90New FriendA random skeleton walks by carrying a scroll. It asks the PCs to buy it a drink at the next village.
91-95Wolf AmbushThe party are being stalked by 1d6+2 wolves. If the party succeeds on a DC 15 Wisdom (Perception) check, they notice the wolves. Otherwise, when the wolves attack, they gain surprise.
96-100Beauty ContestAn oni and a hag are in the middle a clearing, discussing who is prettier. They ask the party to help settle the problem. Consider your answer carefully!


City Random Encounters Table
RollTitleDescription
1-50Nothing HappensThe party travels without interruption.
51-55Urchin MobWhen turning a corner on a busy street, the party is mobbed by approximately 20 children, who run pass, knocking right into the party. There is at least 2 pickpockets in their group. If the party succeeds on a DC15 Wisdom (Perception) check, the notice small hands grabbing their coin pouch.
56-60Strange MerchantPassing an alley the party are called by name from a figure standing in the shadows, offering a small case filled with interesting trinkets. For a small fee he promises a item that is "exactly what they need to get out of a jam in the near future". He's asking for 100gp.
61-65Pet WalkerThe party notices a commotion in the street ahead of them. The crowd of city-goers parts to reveal a Displacer Beast! After a moment of panic you realize that the beast has a collar on, and is being led on a leash. If the party inquires about this strange scene, the walker informs you that they are the majordomo of a local Baron, and is taking his lords pet on a walk
66-70Your Lucky DayAs you step over a puddle, you notice something shiny in the water. Upon investigation you see that it is an ornate knife, with gilding and perhaps even jewels in the hilt. What could be the story with this thing?
71-75Your Unlucky Day(choose a random player)That last battle you were in must have loosened your money-pouch. As you are walking over a bridge mid-town, you feel it drop just as you are taking a step. You don't have enough time to react and inadvertently kick it over the side, where it lands in the water with a splash. Do you go in after it?
76-80Bar BrawlAs you pass by a rowdy saloon, 1d6+2 toughs step out and immediately make eye contact. It looks like they have been drinking and are looking for a fight. Unless you can find some way to calm the situation, you are going to be in a brawl!
81-85Fire SaleA nearby warehouse has burned down, and the merchant has nowhere to store his weapons and armor. It's the rainy season, and he's worried he won't be able to keep up with their upkeep until he can find new storage. He is offering a 30% discount on all steel armor and weapons, today only!
86-90Displacer Beast EscapeFolks are screaming and fleeing all around you. In the commotion you make out the words, "Beast", "Escape", and "Rampage". If you investigate the situation you find a Displacer beast in a town square, obviously out of its element, and enraged. You notice it wears a fancy harness and leash, which seems to have broken.
91-95Thief AmbushThe party are being stalked by 1d6+2 Thieves. If the party succeeds on a DC 15 Wisdom (Perception) check, they notice the Thieves trailing them. Otherwise, they will wait until the players are alone in and alley, and attack with surprise.
96-100The PerformanceThe characters come across an enigmatic street performer who captivates the crowd with an otherworldly performance. The performer's music and dance seem almost magical, drawing in a growing audience. However, as the performance continues, strange things begin to happen: objects float, lights flicker, and a feeling of unease settles over the crowd.
