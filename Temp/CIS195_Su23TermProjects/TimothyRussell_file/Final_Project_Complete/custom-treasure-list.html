Custom Items

Below is a listing of custom items that I have created for various campaigns. Flavor text of each item comes from my homebrew campaign, based off of the Forgotten Realms, but with custom content. These items have been playtested, and I have done my best to remove any over powered elements.

Memorial Scimatar
Weapon(Scimatar)
Rare
Requires Attunement
Created by the Yuan-Ti clerics of Asparis. When the bearer kills a monster with this weapon, treat this weapon as a +1 weapon whenever your fight another monster of that kind. When the weapon is unattuned, it loses its memory.
This weapon is attuned after its first kill. It becomes unattuned if given to another person.
Atai Cloak
Wonderous Item
Uncommon
Requires Attunement
These cloaks are a standard issue to Gensai Peacekeepers of the Atai mountain region. This specific cloak has the name "Teves Flamewise" embroidered on the seam. Once per day, the wearer my use their reaction to reduce fall damage by 1d6.
To attune the cloak must be worn for 8 consecutive hours. If not worn for a period of 5 days, the user loses attunement.
Evesdropping Mug
Wonderous Item
Uncommon
Requires Attunement
A devious device crafted by a jealous dwarven sorcerer from the realm of Amethyst Halls. As long as the mug and owner are on the same plane, the owner can hear through this item as if they are present.
To attune the bearer must drink a full serving of ale or mead out of the mug while another person tells them a secret.
Ring of Ooze Holding
Wonderous Item
Rare
The wizard Gilvan's developed this ring out of neccssity, after finding his tower infested with oozes after a long absense. Once per day the wearer can cast Hold Monster as a 5th level spell on creatures with the OOZE classification. Save vs Wis DC14.
