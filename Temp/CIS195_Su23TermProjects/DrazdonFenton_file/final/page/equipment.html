A brief look at equipment

A look at the equipment used by lower enlisted for their roles.

The Duty belt is the life line of the military police corpman, it contains everything one needs to patrol and keep order. As well as the duty belt, soldiers upholding the law also utilize their stab-proof vest and patrol car to keep the base under order. A large part of the equipment used is used during 'fieldcycle' or when a unit goes to a training ground for days or weeks at a time. Popular MP gear on the field includes the M2A1 .50 Caliber machine gun and the Mk17 Fully Automatic Grenade Launcher kept mounted on a soldiers gun truck or MRAP/MATV (mine-resistant ambush protected (MRAP) vehicle/MRAP All Terrain Vehicle).

"Assist, Protect, Defend"
The United States Army's Military Police provide an important function in the full spectrum of Army operations as a member of the Maneuver, Fires, and Effects division. The Military Police Corps provides expertise in policing, detainment, and stability operations in order to enhance security and enable mobility. Military Police are actively utilized in direct combat and during peacetime.
