Kevin Durant

Coming in at the fifth and final spot is Kevin Durant

Kevin Durant started his NBA career in 2007 when he was drafted 2nd overall by the Seattle Supersonics. Kevin Durant clocks in at nearly 7 feet tall and has the ball handling and shooting skills of a guard which makes him a almost unguardable player. Year after Year Kevin Durant has proven that he is one of the best if not the single best scorer in the league. Kevin has earned the nick name "Slim Reaper" for his very skinny frame.
