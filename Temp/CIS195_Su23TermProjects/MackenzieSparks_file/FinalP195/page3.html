Where to Buy?

Local Shops vs. Online Shops

There are countless places to buy sunflower seeds online. A perk of buying online is there is a vast amount of varieties to choose from. You can find rare and unique flowers that aren't often seen in a everyday garden.

While no one can deny that the internet has so many wonderful options to buy plans, sunflowers are wonderful all on their own, and I personally love supporting local buisinesses. Below I will list some of my favorite local shops here in Eugene, OR.

Down To Earth

Down To Earth is lovely shop located in the Whitaker neighborhood. It opened in 1977 and has a wide assortment of seasonal flowers to add magic to any garden




Johnson Brothers Garden Market

Johnson Brothers is a family favorite! Offering sturdy and well-kept plants year-round, this is a go to spot for plant gifts to share with friends, loved ones, and yourself!




Thistledown Farm

A local treasure, visitors are able to enjoy fresh cut flowers or can shop at their homey nursery. Be it spring, summer, or fall you can find beautiful plants to liven up your garden.
